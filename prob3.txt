✔ Accessibility refers to the practice of designing and developing that can ve accessed and used by people with disabilities or different needs, without any barrier or limitations.

=> There are some devices which play a major role in providing accessibility:

✔ Screen Reader: A software that reads out the content of a web page to individuals, who are visually impaired.
                 It can also interpret and communicate informaion about graphics, multimedia and other element on the page.

✔ Voice Recognition Software: This software enables user to navigate web pages and input text using voice command.
                               It is particularly useful for individuals with mobility impairments or those who have difficulty using a keyboard or mouse.

✔ Keyboard alternatives: This allow individuals with physical disabilities to navigate and interact with web pages without traditional keyborad or mouse.
